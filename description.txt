#**Pawn Party Game**
Pawn Party

##**Overview**
You are a pawn shop owner who is secretly working with the police. 
There are reports of items being stolen in the area and being tracked down to pawn shops. 
Your job is to cath those who you suspect stole the item they brought in.

###**Goal**
In order to make a customer suspect you have to question them about their item. 
The player has to make their choice based off the answers.
Calling the Police on criminals will raise your score.
